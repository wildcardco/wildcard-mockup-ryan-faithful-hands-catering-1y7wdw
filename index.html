Who we are

The name isn't a slogan. It's a promise.

Faithful Hands Catering is a small, locally rooted kitchen on Indianapolis Boulevard in Highland, Indiana. We cook the kind of food people actually go back for seconds of — comfort‑food‑forward menus, built around your gathering, prepared by hands that care how it lands on the plate.

We're not a call center or a franchise dispatch line. When you call, you talk to the people who will plan your menu, cook your meal, and set your buffet. That's how catering works for the families, churches, and small businesses of our community — and it's how we like it.

"Food for a wedding, a reunion, or a memorial should feel like it came from somebody's kitchen — because ours does."
